RPA Development

Saturday, Mar 28, 2020

Information about RPA RPA (Robotic Process Automation) is a technology that allows computer software to be configured as to have a “robot” imitate and integrate the actions of a human being interacting with the digital systems to execute one or several business processes. Is RPA different than other automation tools? RPA allows businesses to automate at a fraction of the cost and takes considerably less time to implement. RPA is non-intrusive in nature and leverages the existing infrastructure without causing disruption to underlying systems, which would be difficult and costly to replace.
